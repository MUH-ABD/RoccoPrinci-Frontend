Our Services

From daily fresh bakes to custom creations — here’s how we serve you.

Artisan Breads

Sourdough, ciabatta, rye, baguettes — slow-fermented breads baked fresh daily, available for pickup.

Custom Cakes

Weddings, birthdays, engagements — design your dream cake with our decorators. 2–7 day notice required.

Pastries & Desserts

Croissants, tarts, brownies, cookies — perfect with coffee or for gifting. Special flavors change weekly.

Catering

Corporate lunches, birthdays, intimate gatherings — we offer sweet & savory packages with delivery.

Workshops

Learn sourdough, cake decorating, or pastry basics in our small-group baking classes with Rocco Princi.

Online Orders

Same-day delivery on selected items in Karachi. Custom orders can be booked via WhatsApp or our form.
